Failback example

In Example 3, in the following figure, the Access Gateway N_1 remains disabled because the corresponding F_A1 port is offline. However, N_2 comes back online. Refer to Failover example for the original failover scenario.

Ports F_1 and F_2 are mapped to N_1 and continue routing to N_3. Ports F_3 and F_4, which were originally mapped to N_2, are disabled and rerouted to N_2, and then enabled.
