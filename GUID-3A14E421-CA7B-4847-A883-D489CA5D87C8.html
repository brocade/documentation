Supporting Fabric OS 7.4.1

Part Number: 53-1003937-02

Upgrade and downgrade considerations for the ADS policy

Downgrading to Fabric OS v6.4.0 or earlier is supported.

Downgrading from Fabric OS v7.1.0 to v6.4.0 or upgrading from Fabric OS v6.4.0 to v7.1.0 will not change the ADS policy settings.
