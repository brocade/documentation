Configure Call Home
===================

Call Home notification allows you to configure |product| to automatically send an email alert to a support center to report system problems on specified devices (such as Fabric OS switches, routers, and directors).

#. Click the **Settings** icon ( |SettingsIcon| ), and then select **Network Monitoring > Call Home**.

#. Click the name of Call Home center you want to configure.

#. In the Call Home center drill-down page, click **Add** and **Remove** to add or remove devices. Click **Configure** to add or remove event filters from selected devices.

   .. image:: graphics/Spectre-CallHomeDrillDown.png

#. Select the **Enable** checkbox to enable the Call Home center.

#. Click **Save**.
