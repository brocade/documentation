Monitoring trunking

For F_Port masterless trunking, you must install Filter, EE, or TT monitors on the F_Port trunk port. Whenever the master port changes, it is required to move the monitor to the new master port. For example, if a master port goes down, a new master is selected from the remaining slave ports. If you attempt to add a monitor to a slave port, it is automatically added to the master port.
